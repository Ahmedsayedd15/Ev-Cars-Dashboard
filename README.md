 🚗 EV Market Analysis Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze the Electric Vehicle (EV) market using data visualization and business intelligence techniques. This project provides insights into sales performance, vehicle specifications, pricing, and customer satisfaction.

📊 Dashboard Overview

This dashboard enables users to explore the EV market through interactive filters and visualizations, helping uncover key trends and business insights.

 Key Features

📈 Sales Trends Over the Years

🚗 Sales Distribution by Brand

💰 Average Vehicle Price by Market Segment

🌍 Vehicle Distribution by Country

🔋 Battery Capacity vs. Driving Range

⚡ Price vs. Horsepower Correlation

⭐ Top 10 Brands by Customer Rating

📊 Vehicle Distribution by Body Type

🎯 Interactive Filters (Year, Brand, Market Segment)

🔄 Reset Filters Button

🛠️ Tools & Technologies

Microsoft Power BI

Power Query

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

Data Modeling

Data Visualization

 📈 Business Insights

The dashboard helps answer questions such as:

Which brands have the highest annual sales?

How do vehicle prices vary across market segments?

Is there a relationship between battery capacity and driving range?

Which countries contribute the most to the EV market?

Which brands receive the highest customer ratings?
